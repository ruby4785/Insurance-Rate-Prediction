Welcome to the Insurance Charges Prediction

This is your one-stop destination for predicting individual medical costs billed by health insurance. This application utilizes various factors to provide you with accurate predictions. Take a moment to understand the key factors that are consider:

Factors:

  • Age: Age of the primary beneficiary.
  • Sex: Gender of the insurance contractor - Female or Male.
  • BMI: Body Mass Index, an objective index of body weight in relation to height (kg/m^2). Ideally between 18.5 to 24.9.
  • Children: Number of children that the individual has.
  • Smoker: Indicates whether the individual is a smoker or not.
  • Region: Residential area in the US - Northeast, Southeast, Southwest, Northwest.

Charges:

Charges: Individual medical costs billed by health insurance.

Enter the information in the form to get predictions tailored to your profile. It's easy, intuitive, and designed to help you plan for your health insurance costs effectively.
